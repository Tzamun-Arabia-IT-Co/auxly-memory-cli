# Personal

## Summary
Private life facts — family, relationships, health, and personal legal/financial
matters. This is your private bucket.

> 🔒 PRIVACY: `personal.md` is OFF by default for remote connections. A remote box
> can only read it if you explicitly share it (Remote tab → highlight the box →
> press `s` → check `personal.md`). Locally, it's always yours to see.

## How to Fill
Tell any connected agent: "Save this to my personal memory." Family, health, and
personal-legal details are also routed here automatically by the memory taxonomy,
and On-Demand Organize will move any such facts found in other files into here.
